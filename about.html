Our Story

We Make It. We Ship It.

GetSmoke was built on one idea: give Canadians direct access to quality native cigarettes at honest prices, with no middleman in between.

Who We Are

Born on the Reserve. Built for Canada.

GetSmoke is a Canadian manufacturer of native cigarettes, operating on First Nations land in Ontario. We grow, process, and package our tobacco products entirely within Canada — and we ship them directly to your door, anywhere across the country.

We started GetSmoke because we were frustrated with the way tobacco was sold in Canada. Layers of resellers, distributor markups, and retail margins meant smokers were paying two or three times what the product actually cost to make. We decided to cut all of that out.

Today, when you order from GetSmoke, you're buying from the people who made your cigarettes — not a store that bought them from someone who bought them from someone else. That's the GetSmoke difference.
