Privacy Policy

How GetSmoke collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: April 2025 · Effective date: April 2025

GetSmoke ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website getsmoke.ca. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or place an order with us. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you place an order or contact us. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Shipping address (street, city, province, postal code)
  • Order details (products ordered, quantities)
  • Payment confirmation (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Messages or notes you include with your order or contact form

We may also automatically collect non-personal information such as browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site through standard web analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send you order confirmations and tracking information
  • Respond to your questions and customer service requests
  • Verify your age as required for tobacco product sales
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with applicable Canadian laws and regulations

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with trusted third parties only as necessary to fulfill your order or operate our business:

  • Shipping carriers (UPS, FedEx, Purolator, Canada Post) — to deliver your order
  • Payment processors — to process your payment securely
  • Form processing services (e.g. Formspree) — to receive and manage order submissions

These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purpose of providing services to us.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by Canadian law. Order records are typically kept for a minimum of 7 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

5. Cookies

Our website uses a session storage item to remember your age verification during your browser session. This is not a tracking cookie and is cleared when you close your browser. We may also use basic analytics cookies to understand site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

6. Age Verification

In compliance with Canadian laws regarding the sale of tobacco products, we require all visitors to confirm they are 19 years of age or older before accessing our website. By confirming your age, you represent that you are of legal tobacco purchase age in your province or territory.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We recommend that you never share sensitive personal information over unsecured channels.

8. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent to the use of your personal information (where applicable)
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at orders@getsmoke.ca.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as shipping carrier tracking pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us:

  • Email: orders@getsmoke.ca
  • Hours: Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm EST
  • Location: Ontario, Canada
